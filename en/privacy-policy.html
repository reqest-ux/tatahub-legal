3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Sync data between your devices
  • Enable sharing features with family members
  • Send important notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app quality and fix bugs
  • Process subscription payments

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase: Cloud infrastructure and authentication
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: Subscription payment processing
  • Family members: Only data you explicitly choose to share

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • User content: Retained until you delete it or your account
  • Logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent: At any time for optional processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at tatahub@capedtech.com.

7. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest on Firebase servers
  • Access-based security rules
  • Secure authentication with Firebase Auth

8. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to Google servers in the United States. Google adheres to standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission to ensure adequate data protection.

9. Children

TataHub is not intended for children under 16 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
